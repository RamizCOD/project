History of the United States Since 1865

Survey of U.S. history from Reconstruction to the present: Reconstruction, Industrial Revolution, Progressive Era politics, problems of 20th century including economic, political, cultural, international and social changes in the modern United States; including 20th century major wars, Depression era, and the Cold War era. Prerequisite: Course requires Reading Placement Test Score-Category One.
